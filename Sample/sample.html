Chicken Adobo

Category: Ulam

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 40 mins | Serves: 4

Tags: Classic, Quick, Budget-friendly

Adobo is the unofficial national dish of the Philippines. Every family has its own twist â€” this one is garlicky, tender, and perfectly balanced with soy sauce and vinegar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup cane vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, smashed
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken with soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorns for at least 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Sear chicken until golden brown.
  3. Pour in the marinade, add vinegar and bay leaves. Simmer.
  4. Add a splash of water if needed. Cook until chicken is tender and sauce is thickened.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Nutrition Info (approx.):

Calories: 380 | Protein: 28g | Carbs: 5g | Fat: 25g
